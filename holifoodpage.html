Thandai is a delicious cooling drink made with milk, flavored with nuts, seeds and spices, and adorned with saffron strands and rose petals. It is enjoyed in the Northern parts of India during the warm summer months and especially on the occasion of Holi. "As a drink, Thandai is believed to have been originated during the mythological era. "It was the favourite drink of Lord Shiva", said Chef and culinary expert Reetu Uday Kugaji. People often mix bhang in thandai, especially on the occasion of Holi, to make it an intoxicating drink.
